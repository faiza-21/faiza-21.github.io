Ways to Take Action

There are lots of ways to contribute to improving homelessness and poverty in your communities.

Here are Some Ways to Take Action:

•Donate money or belongings to local homeless shelters

•Donate food at food drives

•Volunteer at local shelters

•Give away your old clothes to homeless shelters

•Spread awareness! Share what you've learned from this website with others you know. If nothing comes to mind, click on the button below to find out another fact about homelessness/poverty and share that instead.
